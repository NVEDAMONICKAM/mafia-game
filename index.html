How to Play

Objective

Bad Guys want to eliminate all Good Guys.

Good Guys want to eliminate all Bad Guys.

Jesters win if they get voted out.

Winning

Bad Guys win when they outnumber Good Guys (alive).

Good Guys win when all Bad Guys are dead.

Jesters win when any Jester is voted out.

Love (Cupid)

Cupid links a new couple every night.

Love chains grow: A-B night 1, B-C night 2 means A-B-C chain.

If any chain member dies, all in the chain die.

A loved Assassin becomes a Civilian.

Notes
