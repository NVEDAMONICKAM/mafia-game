ℹ️ How to Play

🎯 Objective

Bad Guys want to eliminate all Good Guys.

Good Guys want to eliminate all Bad Guys.

Jesters win if they get voted out.

🏆 Winning

• Bad Guys win when they ≥ Good Guys (alive).

• Good Guys win when all Bad Guys are dead.

• Jesters win the moment any Jester is voted out.

🌙 Night vs ☀️ Day

• Night: Special roles act in secret.

• Day: Players discuss, then vote someone out.

💘 Love (Cupid)

• If a loved player dies, their partner dies too.

• If a loved Assassin falls in love → becomes Civilian.

📝 Notes

• The Spy cannot speak — ever.

• The Survivalist is immune in round 1 only.

• The Gunsmith may give themselves the gun once.

• The Vigilante dies if they shoot an innocent.

• Healer cannot block the Assassin.
